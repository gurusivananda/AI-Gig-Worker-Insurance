# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance for Gig Workers

## 📌 Problem Statement

India’s gig economy delivery partners (Swiggy, Zomato, Zepto, Amazon, etc.) face frequent income loss due to external disruptions such as heavy rain, extreme heat, pollution, and local restrictions.

Currently, there is **no insurance system that protects their daily/weekly income** from such uncontrollable events. As a result, delivery partners can lose up to **20–30% of their earnings**, affecting their financial stability.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

We propose an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that provides **automatic income protection** for delivery partners.

Instead of traditional insurance claims, our system:
- Monitors real-time external conditions (weather, pollution, disruptions)
- Automatically detects income-impacting events
- Instantly triggers payouts without manual claims

---

## 👤 Target Persona

**Persona: Food Delivery Partner (Swiggy/Zomato)**

### Scenario:
Ravi is a delivery partner working in Hyderabad.  
On a particular day:
- Heavy rainfall reduces order demand
- Roads are flooded, limiting movement
- Ravi loses a significant portion of his daily income  

👉 Our platform detects this event and **automatically compensates Ravi** for his income loss.

---

## 🔄 System Workflow

1. User registers on the platform
2. Weekly insurance policy is generated
3. AI calculates premium based on risk factors
4. System continuously monitors external data:
   - Weather conditions
   - Pollution levels
   - Local disruptions
5. If predefined thresholds are crossed:
   - Claim is automatically triggered
6. System verifies authenticity (fraud detection)
7. Instant payout is processed to the user

---

## 💰 Weekly Premium Model

Our platform follows a **weekly subscription-based pricing model**:

- Premium range: ₹30 – ₹100 per week
- Calculated dynamically using:
  - Location risk (flood-prone, heat zones)
  - Historical weather data
  - Average working hours
  - Delivery frequency

### Example:
- Low-risk area → ₹30/week  
- High-risk area → ₹80/week  

---

## ⚡ Parametric Triggers

The system uses predefined conditions (no manual claims required):

| Trigger Type        | Condition Example                  | Impact                |
|--------------------|----------------------------------|----------------------|
| Heavy Rain         | Rainfall > 50mm                  | Reduced deliveries   |
| Extreme Heat       | Temperature > 40°C               | Unsafe working       |
| Pollution          | AQI > 300                        | Health risk outdoors |
| Social Disruption  | Curfew / Area restrictions       | No movement allowed  |

👉 Note: Only **income loss is covered**, not physical damages.

## 🤖 AI/ML Integration

Our system uses AI for:

### 1. Risk Assessment

### 2. Dynamic Pricing

### 3. Fraud Detection
